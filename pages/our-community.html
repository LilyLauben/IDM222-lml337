Our Community:

Just like a party of adventurers, we can’t do this alone! Learn more about our mentors and fellow creators in Philadelphia and beyond.

Drexel AGO

Drexel’s Anime & Gaming Organization (AGO) were some of our original playtesters whose enthusiasm and positive feedback encouraged us to work more on the game. AGO works to bring game enthusiasts together to make new friends and share new experiences through diverse gaming events, and to promote anime within the Drexel community through screenings and discussions. AGO conducts weekly screenings, twice weekly board and tabletop gaming sessions, and all-day long video games streamed live on Twitch! Looking to play video games, tabletop games, or watch anime with like-minded people?

Check out their Facebook group or DragonLink page.

Drexel EGS

The Entrepreneurial Game Studio (EGS) at Drexel University is an experimental and creative game development studio and publisher helping create the next generation of leaders in the Philadelphia game industry. EGS provides a safe and encouraging environment to mold students into entrepreneurs by developing and commercializing their games. They’ve acted as steadfast advisors, playtesters, and supporters ever since Gates of Adventure joined the incubator in 2017.

For more information about EGS, how to join, and how to play student games in addition to Gates of Adventure, check out their website.

Philly GMG

The Game Makers Guild (GMG) is a community of tabletop game developers and players who playtest games to help developers take their ideas from prototypes to reality. Gates of Adventure saw one of its first public playtests at their International Board Game Day in 2017, and the team continue to attend the organization’s monthly playtesting sessions since then. Originally started in Boston, MA, there is now a thriving branch in Philadelphia, which meets the second and fourth Wednesday of every month. The second Wednesday is at Redcap's Corner in Philadelphia, PA, while the fourth Wednesday is at The Games Keep in West Chester, PA.

For more details check out their website. or their Facebook group.
